The Best Day of My Life

I didn't know I could love someone as much as I loved her!

How it started...


I met Haley Price in my freshman ward at BYU during Winter 2020. We started dating just before COVID-19 hit and then, since we couldn't see anyone else, we just kept seeing each other. After dating Haley for a few months, I started to realize what an incredible, sweet, kind, hard working, determined person she is. I literally never wanted to be away from her!

How it's going...


After dating for about 10 months, I proposed to Haley on New Years Eve 2020 and we got married on April 30th, 2021 in the Payson Utah Temple. This was the best day of my life because I got to marry my best friend :)
