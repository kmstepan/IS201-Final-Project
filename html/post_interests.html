My Interests

Sports, Adventure, SCUBA, and more...

Sports

Playing and watching sports has been a big part of my life since I was little. It was a way for my family to bond and have fun. Growing up, I always played basketball with my dad and brothers. This led me to play basketball throughout highschool. I also played baseball and golf in high school. Outside of school, I loved playing flag football with friends, kickball with my siblings, and recently, I've really enjoyed learning to play pickleball. Exercise is also really important to me. I especially enjoy running, weightlifting, HIIT, and swimming. When I'm not playing sports, I'm watching them, especially the Golden State Warriors.

Adventures


I've had the opportunity to go on some incredible adventures throughout my life. From trekking 50 miles through Yosemite to cycling all around the Bay Area, I really enjoy getting outdoors. My wife and I like to hike and camp, but my favorite adventures always involve SCUBA diving. I learned to dive when I was 17 and I recently picked it back up through a course at BYU. I have done dives around Catalina Island in California, Sand Hollow Reservoir in St. George, Utah, and the Homestead Crater in Midway, UT.

SCUBA



Like I said before, I love to SCUBA dive. If you'd like to learn more about SCUBA, visit the PADI website, one of the leading SCUBA certification and training organizations in the world.
